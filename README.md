Hi 👋 My name is Dimi
=====================

Aspiring Angular Frontend Developer
-----------------------------------

Hello there! I'm currently immersed in the world of programming, dedicating myself full-time since September 2023 to mastering the craft. What initially sparked my journey was the desire to create a user-friendly website for my family's restaurant, where patrons could conveniently pre-order and pay for their meals. Prior to delving into the realms of coding, I spent six semesters studying German and Spanish in Bielefeld. However, I found the allure of programming far more captivating and decided to pivot towards this path—a decision I've never looked back on. Each day, I embrace the challenges and triumphs of this dynamic field with enthusiasm and determination.

* 🌍  I'm based in Dortmund
* ✉️  You can contact me at [dimitrioskapetanis1996@gmail.com](mailto:dimitrioskapetanis1996@gmail.com)
* 🧠  I'm learning Angular & Typescript
